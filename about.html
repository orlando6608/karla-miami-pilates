About Karla

Pilates by Karla

Pilates is not just movement — it’s connection, control and confidence.

My name is Karla, a certified Pilates instructor based in Miami, specializing in strength, mobility, and mindful movement.

I work with clients of all levels, helping them build long-term body awareness, stability, and confidence through intelligent and personalized Pilates training.

I currently teach at multiple Pilates studios across Miami and also offer private sessions with flexible locations agreed directly with my clients.
