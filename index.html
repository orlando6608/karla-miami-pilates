About Karla

Karla is a certified Pilates instructor based in Miami, specializing in strength, mobility, and mindful movement. She works with clients of all levels, helping them build long-term body awareness, stability, and confidence through intelligent and personalized Pilates training.

She currently teaches at several Pilates studios across Miami and focuses on creating balanced, effective sessions that support both physical performance and overall well-being.
